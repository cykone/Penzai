About Us

Supporting people to find roles that really fit them

And while most organizations and job platforms still try to find and match people based on their past, we thought: "Isn't it much more natural to ask where do you want to go, instead of where have you been?".

Our Mission

What we do

PENZAI changes how organizations find talent. Instead of focusing on people’s experiences, PENZAI understands peoples’ aspirations and personal development. It leverages this knowledge to connect the right candidates with businesses that will support their aspirations.

Why

PENZAI believes that companies have to understand themselves as a service provider for their workforce; this includes the support to meet the learning aspirations of their employees. But today, many organizations still understand learning and developing new competencies as a cost factor and will only be supported as long as it is relevant to the current job description. PENZAI wants to help people to find their passion, make a career change easy, and match them with organizations that support their aspirations.
