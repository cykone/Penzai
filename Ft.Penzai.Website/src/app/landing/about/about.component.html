About Us

Supporting people to find and grow into roles that really fit to them.

PENZAI is in an early stage of changing the way of how people find new challenges. We believe that work must be about us as individuals and especially about our personal goals and aspirations. And while most organisations and job platforms still try to find and match people based on their past, we thought: "Isn't it much more natural to ask where do you want to go, instead of where have you been?".

Our Mission

By understanding the current situation and future goals of our members, we provide them with the information and the support they need to grow into a role that suits them.

What we do

We assess the current situation of the user with a short questionnaire about his personality, preferred work environment, motivation and competencies.

Based on this profile our algorithms are able to find profiles, roles and skills of people with similar a similar background. This helps our users to expand their horizons outside their current boundaries, to see what roles might fit them.

Once they know where they want to start growing into, we provide them with information relevant to their goals.

We track the activities and the success of our members. Based on this information we are able to match them with our industry partners who provide access to more learning opportunities and hands-on experience.
