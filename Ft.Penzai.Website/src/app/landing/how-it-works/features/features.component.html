Features

PENZAI gives you the tools to grow and access to our industry partners

While you are learning, PENZAI grows with you and learns about your learning style, preferences and interests. It helps you to navigate your growth journey by personalising your learning experience. Based on this knowledge, PENZAI is able to connect you with sandbox projects from industry partners which are waiting to support your journey

On the Learning dashboard you can manage your growth process easily

  • Upcoming events and meetups in your local area, which are relevant to your current learning path.
  • Get an overview of the best rated and valuable online courses and enroll directly to them.
  • Manage your enrolled courses and the events you want to attend.
  • Check your results and endorsements. See what you achieved and probably what you didn't like.
The community brings people together to help each other to learn and grow

  • Something is stopping you from making progress? You need help with this one thing you don't get. Ask questions and find answers in the community.
  • The community is separated in different topic groups with interests sub channels. For instance, you want to ask a question about blockchain. Checkout the blockchain channel in the distributed ledger focus group.
  • Subscribe to specific channels and get updates immediately by notification and/or email.
Our industry partners define the brief. You work on it.

  • Get invites to apply sandbox projects and briefs defined be real businesses out there.
  • Once accepted, you get the real hands on experience of what the jobs feels like.
  • Set up the roles, skills and industries you are interested in.
  • Collect endorsements and promotions into the orbit of the organisation, which means from now on you can get invites to work on real projects with them.
