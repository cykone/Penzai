Privacy

What kind of personal data do you collect?

PENZAI collects contact details of users including Firstname, Lastname and the Email Address.

Why does PENZAI collect this data and what is it doing with it?

PENZAI will never share any information with any other parties. This website and the member area is part of a student project. PENZAI uses the collected data for validating the proposition, by users signing up.

What about all the other data I am entering?

PENZAI is just a prototype and done for validating an idea. All data, except your contact details, is temporary. The data is wiped as soon as you refresh your browser window or you leave the page. (F5) This does not include the contact details you entered when signing up to PENZAI.

How do I delete my account/data

Please login to your account. Under your profile, you can find a button 'delete account'
